# Licensing for the mooR Book

The mooR Book is licensed separately from the mooR software.

The book contains a mixture of:

- original mooR documentation
- adapted and derived material from earlier MOO documentation
- credited historical material incorporated under the notice reproduced in `src/legal.md`

Because of that mixed provenance, the book is not covered by the repository's software license.

## Terms

Subject to preservation of the applicable copyright and permission notices:

- You may make and distribute verbatim copies of the book.
- You may copy and distribute modified versions of the book.
- You may copy and distribute translations of the book.

Modified versions and translations must be distributed under a permission notice identical to the
notice reproduced in [src/legal.md](./src/legal.md), except that a translated permission notice may
be used where the notice itself permits that.
